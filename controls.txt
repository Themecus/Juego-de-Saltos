Espacio para saltar

Shift para correr
Puedes hacer un salto con una carrera, solo mantén la carrera y presiona el salto, ten cuidado, no mantengas el salto durante la carrera-salto, solo mantén la carrera y presiona el salto.
TAB para salir en cualquier momento del juego.

A, D to move
Space to jump
Space again when you see a wall to do another jump, stick to it and then press
Shift to run
You can do a jump with a run, just hold the run and press the jump, be careful, don't hold the jump during the run-jump, just hold the run and press the jump.
TAB to exit the game at any time.
You can take another jump by stepping on enemies.
